About Yardman Finds

Yardman Finds is a content‑first, AI‑powered deal curation service built for the modern, trend‑savvy consumer. Our brand is rooted in the idea of digging for value: we search the web and unearth hidden gems so you don’t have to. Forget scrolling endlessly—let the Yardman do the heavy lifting.

We’ve embraced our quirky name to stand out in a crowded space. Rather than leaning on predictable buzzwords, Yardman Finds feels like a friend who knows the inside scoop. We combine a savvy voice with a promise: if there’s an incredible deal out there, we’ll find it and share it with our community.

Zero‑Budget, Profit‑First Model

Manual Content Engine

We start by creating viral content on TikTok, Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts using nothing more than a smartphone and free tools. Each short video highlights one incredible deal and directs viewers to our link hub.

Affiliate Revenue

Our link‑in‑bio points to curated deals via affiliate programs like Amazon Associates. Every purchase made through our unique links earns us a commission—this is how we generate profit without any upfront costs.

Reinvest for Scale

Once the first commissions roll in, we re‑invest profits to buy a domain, pay for professional tools like Webflow or n8n, and eventually build our own AI engine. The business pays for its own growth.

Roadmap

Week 1: First Commission

  • Create social accounts and sign up for affiliate programs.
  • Curate your first deal, generate an affiliate link and update your link hub.
  • Film and post a short video each day; engage with every comment.

Reinvestment Milestones

  • €20: Register the yardmanfinds.com domain.
  • €100: Subscribe to professional tools like Webflow/Framer & n8n.
  • €200: Allocate budget for the OpenAI API to build the Deal Hunter AI.
  • Ongoing: Fund marketing experiments and pay yourself a salary as profits grow.

AI & Technology

  • Stage 1 – Manual AI: You do the research; free AI tools inspire content ideas.
  • Stage 2 – Semi‑Automated: Use n8n to monitor trends and send daily prompts.
  • Stage 3 – Fully Automated: Build the Deal Hunter AI to scrape, score and populate high‑value deals on your website.
